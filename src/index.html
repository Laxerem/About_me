Yougorsk

I was born in a small town that appeared relatively recently (1962), but I think this city is one of the best small towns in Russia.
The town is developing faster and faster: New roads, parks, and playgrounds are being built.

There are many interesting people in this city who are ready to change the world around them and make the city a better place.

Also, this city is quite safe, and is perfect for families and their children.
