Programming

I don't consider myself a real programmer, but I have some experience.

I discovered programming at the age of 13, the decision to become a programmer came to me suddenly, and I still can't explain exactly what made me do it, maybe I wanted to become like my brother.

So that you can understand how strange it is, at that time, I was studying at a music school, and everyone thought I was going to become a musician.

I like it when I see the result of my work, and even though I often feel like I'm learning very slowly, I realize that I'm getting better.

Music

I've been listening to music since I was a kid, and if I like music, I can imagine it in a variety of situations, which is why music sounds in almost all my videos: I adjust the video to it.

If there were no music, I wouldn't be interested in making videos.

I started learning to play a musical instrument at the age of 11, the first year I went to a small amateur music group, later, my mother enrolled me in a music school, where I studied for 4 years.

Unfortunately, I couldn't finish my studies at a music school - I went to college, but to this day I'm learning new compositions, developing my skills.
